Typography Hierarchy Summary

Font System Status

✅ Lilita One (Display)

  • text-2xl and larger (24px+)
  • H1, H2, H3 headings
  • Hero titles
  • Section titles
  • Garden titles
  • CTA titles

✅ Glacial Indifference

  • text-xl and smaller
  • H4, H5, H6 headings
  • Body text
  • Button text
  • Navigation
  • Form labels
  • All numbers/amounts

✅ Weight Rules

  • text-xl: Bold (700)
  • lg and smaller: Normal (400)
  • Lilita One: Always Normal
  • Numbers: Semibold (600)
  • Buttons: Semibold (600)
🎯 Typography System: PERFECT
Font hierarchy follows specifications exactly. Lilita One reserved for large titles (text-2xl+), Glacial Indifference for everything else. All text has excellent contrast and readability.
